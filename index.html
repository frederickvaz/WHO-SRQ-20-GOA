WHO SRQ-20 App - Goa

Simple Scale for Psychological Distress

The WHO SRQ-20 is a screening tool developed by the World Health Organization to identify psychiatric disturbance. This version has been adapted for the Goan population in Konkani.

Instructions:

  • Answer YES or NO to each question based on the last 30 days.
  • A YES = 1 and NO = 0.
  • A total score of 8 or more indicates psychiatric disturbance.
  • Confidentiality is assured. Be honest with your responses.
